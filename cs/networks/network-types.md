* Body (BAN)
* Personal (PAN)
* Near-me (NAN)
* Local (LAN)
  * Home (HAN)
  * Storage (SAN)
  * Wireless (WLAN)
* Campus (CAN)
* Backbone
* Metropolitan (MAN)
* Wide (WAN)
* Cloud (IAN)
* Internet
* Interplanetary Internet
